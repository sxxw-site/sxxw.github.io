Your footprints should not just be a "passive cloud connection of points"

The value of recording daily trails is not about "having many points", but rather being able to review, organize, and analyze . TimeTrails turns your movements into a clear timeline : where you went today, how long you stayed, how far you walked, all easily understood at a glance. At the same time, we insist on local priority and minimizing collection —you record to understand yourself, not to be understood by the platform.

What will you use it for

  • Automatically generate 'Today's Trail' every day: route, mileage, and stop points at a glance.
  • Review a trip: Browse your action timeline by day/location
  • Personal review: long-term changes in commuting, exercise, and travel patterns
  • Recall a certain day: quickly locate "that store", "that road", "that walk" on the timeline.
  • Track your pace: trends in commuting/exercise/travel mileage, long-term comparisons are more intuitive.
