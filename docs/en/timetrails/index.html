Local First · Privacy Controlled

Your footprints should not just be "a series of points passively moving to the cloud"

The value of recording daily trajectories is not about "having many points", but rather about being able to review, organize, and reflect . TimeTrails turns your movements into a clear timeline : where you went today, how long you stayed, how far you walked, all easily understood at a glance. At the same time, we insist on local priority and minimizing data collection — you record to understand yourself, not to be understood by the platform.

Local Priority Permissions can be revoked Timeline + Map Staypoint Recognition Exportable

Local saving and processing by default; whether to enable synchronization, whether to export, and to whom to export - all controlled by you.

What will you use it for

  • Automatically generate "Today's Trail" every day: route, mileage, and stop points at a glance.
  • Review a trip: Browse your action timeline by day/location
  • Personal review: long-term changes in commuting, exercise, and travel patterns
  • Recall a certain day: quickly locate "that store", "that road", "that walk" on the timeline.
  • Track your pace: trends in commuting/exercise/travel mileage, long-term comparisons are more intuitive.
