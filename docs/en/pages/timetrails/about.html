About TimeTrails

A more 'locally focused' tool for tracking and recording memories
▾

TimeTrails aims to help you turn information like "where you've been, how long you've stayed, and how far you've traveled" into a personal timeline that is viewable, organized, and exportable. Our core philosophy is: user controllocal firstminimize collection.

What We Do

  • Automatic/semi-automatic recording of routes, waypoints, and mileage statistics
  • Organize daily travel into a timeline and map visualization
  • Supports export (e.g., GPX/CSV, if you enable it or if your version supports it)

Why We Do It

Most "life recording" products are either overly cloud-based or overly social. TimeTrails is more like your personal sailing log: recording is for reflection, organization, and understanding oneself, not for being fed by algorithms.

Privacy and Data Principles

  • Local First: By default, data is stored on your device.
  • Sync optional: will only use iCloud or self-built services for syncing when you actively enable it.
  • permissions can be revoked: You can revoke location, motion, photo, and other permissions at any time in the system settings.
  • does not sell data: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

For detailed terms, please refer to Privacy Policy.

Possible Permissions Requested

  • Location: is used for tracking and waypoints.
  • Sports and Fitness: for more accurate motion recognition.
  • photo: is used only when you import/link photos.
  • Notification: is used to remind and synchronize status prompts (optional).
